FAQ

Have Any Questions?

We are happy to work with entrepreneurs of any scale. Our mission is to
help you set your own business goals and move successfully towards them
with open eyes.

Who are the coaches?

Our coaches are experienced top managers, department heads, and founders of well-
known companies.

Can I buy only one personal consultation?

Yes, you can find the pricing plan below or just contact us to make an appointment
with one of our coaches.

What do your courses consist of?

We offer intensive min-courses in basic business, financial, marketing, and other
disciplines. Each course includes theory, practice, and real case studies from our
coaches.

What should I choose if I'm only starting my business?

We suggest you signing up for our Strategy & Planning course and have 2-3 one-on-
one sessions with our coach.
